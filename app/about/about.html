About Kristi Schmitt

I am married and live in Newport Beach and a mom to our three adult children. I have had my share of struggles and I am so grateful for the healing and shifts I have made in my life and truly want to help others live a life of meaning, fulfillment and purpose.

In my free time, I enjoy paddle boarding, kayaking, hiking, spin cycling, yoga, bridge, games, reading and walking and talking with family and friends.

I am the Care Coordinator and a Care Counselor at Mariner’s church. I absolutely love it. I am always amazed at the difference I see in people lives. It’s so wonderful to see women become the person God designed them to be.

Coaching Qualifications: I am trained as a Coactive Coach – a program certified by ICF – the governing body of life coaches. In addition, I am a certified Intuitive Eating Coach and a certified MELT instructor. I have also had extensive care counseling training on helping people through life’s hardships and difficulties. Finally, I am currently going through a three-year training in Somatic Experiencing – a therapy that helps people with trauma and stress.

My mission is to help women live joyful, fulfilling and meaningful lives.

I would be honored to walk along side of you to help you live your best life.
