What is Life Coaching?

Life Coaching is a powerful and dynamic partnership between coach and client that focuses on who you can be, what you want to create in your life, and support to help you make meaningful changes. Coaching helps remind you that you are the leader in your life. It takes courage, guidance and intention to live a fulfilled life.

Your life may be missing something: time for yourself, peace, fulfillment, balance or passion. Or you may have too much of something else: stress, loss, procrastination, confusion, financial pressure etc. Whatever your life needs, I can offer passionate and personalized support as you work to connect to who you truly are and create the life you always imagined.

Life Coaching is designed to encourage those who feel their lives are in transition; those who are feeling stuck, frustrated and discontent, but know they are capable of so much more. Those who want to know how to trust themselves and master the art of living their dream life.

I will work with you and your unique situation to help you:  
  •  Create a vision of your ideal life by gaining improved clarity about what really matters to you.
  •  Through life’s difficulties and hardships leading you to greater compassion, wisdom and joy
  •  Replace old negative habits, beliefs and self-talk with new ones that serve you better.
  •  Overcome obstacles, procrastination & overwhelm.
  •  Recognize what your top priorities are and learn how to manage your time better so you can become more organized, productive and efficient.
  •  Live an emotionally and physically healthy life.
  •  Increase your self-confidence and become the person you were made to be.
  •  Find increased meaning and satisfaction in your life.  

I offer a caring and non-judgmental environment of support and encouragement - helping you get crystal clear about what you really want now.
